My Projects

Here are a bunch of projects I worked upon as part of my Bachelors Degree and my interests.

Aviation Safety System

The Aviation Safety System project enhances aviation safety by digitizing the collection and analysis of flight accident data. With a user-friendly interface, visualization tools, it empowers stakeholders to access critical safety insights, fostering collaboration and innovation. By integrating machine learning algorithms for predictive safety analysis, the project supports proactive safety management.This project has been made aiming to ensure safer skies worldwide.

TMIT Motor Test 2023

The Motor Test Project undertaken by team ThrustMIT 2023-24 aims to test and analyse the working and response of a Commercial M-Class Solid Rocket Motor. As a part of the Avionics subsystem we undertook the task of ensuring robust and continuous communication between the Ground and the Motor Stations.
